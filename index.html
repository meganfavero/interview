An Interview with

Nutta Sukprasert

By Megan Favero

What do you want to design?

I have bachelor’s degree in fine arts so I want to combine fine arts and graphic design together. So many people think that fine art is something that hard to understand and reach. If I can communicate fine art with graphic design then everybody can get that.

What is difficult or frustrating for you within design and how do you work to push past that?

I’m doing so many assignment in the class and sometime I use the same colour matching for many classes as same as a typeface. After I realized that I try to match new colour that I never use before.

What do you see yourself doing after you finish this program?

It’s hard to think about that now but I hope that I can find a job here as a graphic designer. I already had 4 years experience back in my country about teaching (Jewelry Design and drawing) but graphic design is new challenge for me.

What excites you about design?

I always excited to learn new program and push myself to design something that I never try before. I try so hard to go beyond my own expectation.

What worries you about design?

For now, doing all designs are fun and so challenge however, I still worried when I work with a client is not only design what I think is the best, but also can match with the concept from client.

Can design change the world?

Yes, I think it can make a really big change. Art and design are a media for communication between people and it is more national than language. Designers design something to response the demanding of human that always innovate technology, create theory and design product to make life more comfortable.

What is a website?

In my opinion, It is an online platform that link people together. It’s an interactive platform that we use to communicate to each other. It can be text that people want to pronounce or campaign to help other people. Actually, it can be anything that we use to communicate with other people around the world.
